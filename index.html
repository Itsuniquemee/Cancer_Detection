About CancerDetect AI

Our Mission

Making advanced breast cancer screening accessible through AI technology. Early detection saves lives — our system assists healthcare professionals in identifying potential malignancies faster.

The Technology

Built on the sklearn breast cancer dataset with 30 diagnostic features. Our model extracts texture, shape, and contour metrics from images and classifies them using a trained Logistic Regression model.
